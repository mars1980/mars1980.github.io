Professor Moore's Homepage

I grew up in South Carolina but have lived in Chicago, Los Angeles, and New York. I just graduated from NYU where I studied art, design, and technology. Before I got my Masters degree, I lived in Los Angeles as an artist and part time office manager. I went to undergraduate at Antioch University LA, where I studied art and philosophy. Since moving to New York, I've worked on hardware development projects for artists and even a hydroponics start up. I've also worked freelance as a videographer and technology consultant. I currently am a research fellow at NYU's Interactive Telecommunications Program and an Adjunct Professor at BMCC.

I love hiking mountains, geology, space, and especially mars. In fact, my middle name is Marcelle which is Greek for of Mars. Most of my friends are serious nerds or make fun of me for being such a nerd.
